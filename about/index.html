Deep Roots in Fullerton.

What began in 1944 as a modest poultry farm in the heart of Orange County has blossomed into a global cornerstone of the agricultural industry. For nearly eight decades, Hidden Villa Ranch has remained family-owned, rooted in the rich soil of Fullerton, California.

Our journey from a local supplier to a worldwide leader is a testament to our unwavering commitment to the principles of integrity, innovation, and community. We don't just produce food; we nurture a heritage of excellence that feeds millions across continents.
